Track employee presence with precision using GPS-verified check-in and check-out. Employees capture a timestamped selfie at each check-in to verify physical presence, while the app records GPS coordinates to confirm they are within designated office premises. Automatic leave detection prevents accidental attendance entries during sanctioned leave periods. HR managers and administrators gain visibility into real-time attendance status and can generate detailed attendance reports with a single tap.
Employees can submit leave requests in seconds – select full day or half-day leave, choose the date range, specify the leave type (paid, sick, unpaid), and provide optional notes. The app supports automatic leave balances that update in real-time based on approved requests and organizational policies. Managers and HR teams receive instant notifications of pending leave requests and can approve or reject applications directly from the app. Employees get immediate feedback on request status, creating transparency and reducing back-and-forth communication.

Understanding compensation is now transparent and accessible – no more waiting for printed payslips. Admins can manage payroll configurations, generate payroll reports, and ensure accurate salary processing. Historical payroll records are always available for reference or tax filing purposes.
Assign, manage, and track work tasks with built-in task management. Create tasks with descriptions, due dates, priority levels, and assigned team members. Break down complex projects into subtasks for better organization. Track progress in real-time and receive notifications when tasks are updated, completed, or approaching deadlines. The consolidated task dashboard ensures nothing falls through the cracks.
Stay connected with your team through secure, in-app messaging. Send direct messages to colleagues, create group chats for team coordination, and share files and photos without leaving the app. Chat is fully encrypted and complies with organizational data policies. Search chat history, pin important messages, and maintain clear communication trails for accountability.
Administrators can broadcast company-wide announcements, policy updates, and important notices. Employees receive instant notifications and can access all announcements within the app. This ensures critical information reaches everyone immediately, eliminating the risk of miscommunication or missed updates.

HR Managers review attendance records, process leave requests, and manage team performance. Administrators configure system settings, manage user accounts, and generate organizational reports. This tiered approach ensures data security while providing appropriate access to each role.
